# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is a Power BI reporting solution designed
to analyze employee workforce data, understand attrition patterns, and
provide HR stakeholders with actionable insights for improving employee
retention and workforce planning.

The dashboard presents key HR metrics through interactive
visualizations, enabling analysis of employee demographics, job roles,
salary distribution, tenure, education, and satisfaction levels.

## Project Objectives

-   Monitor overall workforce health and employee demographics
-   Analyze employee attrition trends and identify key influencing
    factors
-   Understand attrition patterns across:
    -   Age groups
    -   Gender
    -   Education background
    -   Job roles
    -   Salary ranges
    -   Employee tenure
-   Support data-driven HR decision-making

## Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard provides high-level workforce metrics including:

-   Total Employee Count
-   Total Attrition
-   Attrition Rate
-   Average Employee Age
-   Average Salary
-   Average Years of Service

### Attrition Analysis

The report includes detailed attrition analysis using:

-   **Attrition by Education**
    -   Identifies education groups with higher employee turnover
-   **Attrition by Age**
    -   Highlights age segments contributing to attrition
-   **Attrition by Salary**
    -   Analyzes relationship between compensation levels and employee
        exits
-   **Attrition by Job Role**
    -   Identifies roles experiencing higher workforce movement
-   **Attrition by Tenure**
    -   Shows employee retention trends based on years of service
-   **Attrition by Gender**
    -   Compares attrition distribution across workforce segments

### Employee Satisfaction Analysis

A job satisfaction matrix is included to evaluate satisfaction levels
across different job roles and identify potential retention risks.
